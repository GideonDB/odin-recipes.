Super Sweet Spaghetti

That is my Spaghetti.

I am showcasing how to make my spaghetti today, we're going to go in detail on how to make this spaghetti. If you love spaghetti, like everyone else that has a brain and tastebuds, you're going to love this spiced up dish.

Ingredients

In this section, we are quickly going to cover the ingredients, that way you can get cookin for yourself as soon as possible, or perhaps your following along, if so perfect!

  • Spaghetti
  • Ground beef
  • Tomato Sauce
  • Butter
  • Pepper
  • Onion Powder
  • Chili Flakes
  • Fresh Basil
  • Olive Oil
  • Salt
  • Parmesan and Mozzarella Cheese

Finally, we are going to run through the steps!

  1. Mediem sized pot of boiling water, place on stove and begin heating.
  2. Into the boiling water, we are going to add a drizzle of Olive Oil, and a hint of salt!
  3. Once the water is boiling, we are going to add the Spaghetti.
  4. Let the pasta boil for about 15 minutes
  5. While the pasta is boiling, get a medium non-stick pan; add half a pound of ground beef, into the skillet, turn the stove onto medium heat.
  6. Using a spatula, break up the ground beef, once the ground beef is half cooked we are going to add the spices, salt, black pepper, and onion powder. After, make sure to cook the ground beef until it is no longer pink.
  7. At this point the pasta should be finished, now, strain the pasta to get the water out, then put the pasta back into the boiling pot.
  8. Add in, two slices of butter, and put the stove onto medium heat, stir the pasta into the butter, once the butter has melted, then we will add the pasta sauce.
  9. Stir everything, making sure everything is coated, then we will add in the ground beef.
  10. Mix the ground beef thoroughly, then add some fresh basil and the chili flakes.
    11. Once everything is mixed together, we can add parmesan and mozzerella cheese too the top, after this we are ready to serve.

    Hopefully, this will help some of you to start cooking Spaghetti, have fun and enjoy!
